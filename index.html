About The Truck

Weekend at Baudy's is a mobile kitchen, where you can enjoy delicious Nicaraguan dishes at your favorite locations in St. Louis.
This food truck's mission is to bring coworkers, friends, family, and new friends together with authentic Nicaraguan-style food with a kick .
Come chase the flavor and drool over the aroma of our Strip Steak and Pork Banh Mis with a side of Elote with your colleagues and friends!
Come support the truck and share extraordinary experiences with other locals!
